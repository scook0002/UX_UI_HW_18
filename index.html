My Skills

User Research

My approach to User Research is to always remain curious and to not enter the research with assumptions. Remaining open and curious allows me to absorb user insights like a sponge, which I can then use to guide my design process. During User Research, I use Miro, Google Drive, Documents, and Surveys to record and analyze user insights.

User Empathy

My approach to User Empathy is to put the user at the center of attention. When I can solely focus on the user, I can gain a deep understandi g of who they are and what is important to them. By empathizing with the user, I am better able to design digital experiences that exceed expectations, while meeting the users' wants, needs, and desires.

Qualitative Interviews

My approach to Qualitative Interviews is to get into the mind of the user. I want to have a conversation as if I were talking with a friend to gain a deeper connection and understanding of my user. During Qualitative Interviews, it is essential that I create an Interview Script with questions that are open to facilitate an honest conversation.
