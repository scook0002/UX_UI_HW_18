My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to User Research is to always remain curious and to not enter the research with assumptions.

Remaining open and curious allows me to absorb user insights like a sponge, that I can then use to guide my design process.

During User Research, I use Miro, Google Drive, Documents, and Surveys to record and analyze user insights.

User Empathy

My approach to User Empathy is to put the user at the center of attention.

When I can solely focus on the user, I can gain a deep understanding of who they are and what is important to them.

By empathizing with the user, I am better able to design a digital experience that exceeds their expectations, as well as meets their wants, needs, and desires.

Qualitative Interviews

My approach to Qualitative Interviews is to get into the mind of the user.

I want to have a conversation as if I were talking to a friend to gain a deeper connection and deeper insight into what the user actually thinks.

During Qualitative Interviews, it is important that I create an Interview Script with questions that are not leading but instead facilitate an open and honest conversation.
